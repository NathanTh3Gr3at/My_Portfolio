Thyme To Cook Recipe App

Thrid Year Group Project

Description

A recipe app designed to simplify meal planning and grocery shopping. Thyme To Cook allows users to search for recipes, save favorites, generate shopping lists, and plan meals efficiently. Built using Flutter and Firebase, the app offers a seamless experience for both casual cooks and food enthusiasts.
